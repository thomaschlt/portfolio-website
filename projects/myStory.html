Website

This project is an interactive French story website that uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. The website features adaptations of famous French books, with the ability for an admin to enter and manage multiple stories. Users can play the stories and make choices that affect the outcome of the game, with the ability to win or lose. At the end of the game, users can view their choices and the impact they had on the story. This website aims to provide a unique and engaging way for users to experience and interact with French literature.
